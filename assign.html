PERSONAL LIBRARY

19 May, 2018

A personal library is a library under the care of private ownership, as compared to that of a public institution, and is usually only established for the use of a small number of people, or even a single person. As with public libraries, some people use bookplates – stamps, stickers or embossing – to show ownership of the items.
Personal Library is a cozy place where anyone can spend a great time reading all his/her favourite books.
